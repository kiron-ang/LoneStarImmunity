Lone Star Immunity

How many seventh graders in your school are vaccinated?

This website uses data from the Texas Department of State Health Services. Every school year, the DSHS surveys schools to learn what percentage of seventh graders are vaccinated. Click the buttons below to make bar charts for different counties and vaccines! Click a bar to learn more about the school district that it represents. The dotted line represents a vaccination rate of 85%; the white bars need to be above this line to make sure that the seventh graders in the school have "herd immunity". Make as many bar charts as you want, and contact Kiron Ang at kiron_ang1@baylor.edu for any questions, comments, or feedback!
